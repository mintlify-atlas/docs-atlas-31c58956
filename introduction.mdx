## What is Nanolytics?

Nanolytics is a minimalistic, zero-dependency lightweight client-side analytics tracking library. It allows you to capture events, store them locally, and submit them to a server after a predefined threshold. The library focuses on simplicity and efficiency, allowing developers to easily integrate event tracking into their applications with minimal overhead.

## Why use Nanolytics?

### Tiny bundle size

Nanolytics is incredibly lightweight. The entire library adds minimal overhead to your application, making it perfect for projects where every kilobyte counts. With zero dependencies, you won't be pulling in unnecessary code.

### Simple and focused

Unlike heavyweight analytics platforms, Nanolytics does one thing well: capturing and batching events. You maintain full control over how and where events are submitted, making it easy to integrate with any backend API.

### Works offline

Events are stored in localStorage, so even if your users lose connectivity, their interactions are captured and will be submitted when you call your submission function.
